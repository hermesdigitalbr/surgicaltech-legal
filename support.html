Frequently asked questions

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
Open Settings on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → Surgical Tech CST Exam Prep. You can cancel anytime; access continues until the end of the billing period.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
Open Surgical Tech CST Exam Prep, go to Settings inside the app and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple Account used for the purchase.

How do I request a refund?
Purchases are handled by Apple. Request a refund at reportaproblem.apple.com.
